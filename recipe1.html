Paneer Tikka

Prep Time: 40 Minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer cubes, Yogurt, Ginger-garlic paste
  • Tikka masala, Bell peppers, Onions

Instructions

  1. Marinate paneer and veggies in yogurt and spices for 20 mins.
  2. Thread onto skewers.
  3. Grill or pan-fry until charred.
